Overview

Red frangipani is also known as Plumeria Rubra. It is a tropical flowering tree native to Central America, Mexico. It is famous for its vibrant pink, red, or reddish-orange flowers with a sweet fragrance. The tree thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil. Its flowers are symbolic in many cultures and commonly used in leis, perfumes, and decorations. The tree can grow up to 5-8 meters tall and requires minimal maintenance, making it popular among gardeners.

Benefits:

With this plant's fragrance, it is beneficial for humans.

  • The sap and flowers are sometimes used for treating skin conditions like ulcers and wounds.
  • In some cultures, it is used as a natural remedy for constipation. In short, it is beneficial for our health.
  • Its vibrant flowers enhance the aesthetic appeal of gardens, homes, and public spaces.
  • It also acts as an air purifier.

Plant's Journey:

  • Right Location

    Frangipani thrives in tropical and untropical climates. It grows well in temperatures between 20 to 40 degrees Celsius.

  • Planting Process

    Dig a hole twice as wide and deep as the root ball. Mix garden soil with sand and compost to improve drainage and nutrients. Water slightly to wet the soil. Avoid overwatering.

  • Watering

    Water sparingly when soil is dry. Water once a week during summer and reduce watering in the monsoon and winter.

  • Blooming

    The tree typically starts blooming after 2-3 years of planting if grown from cuttings. Red frangipani (Red Champa) flowers bloom from spring to autumn.

Leaves and Flowers:

Leaves: The leaves of this plant are elliptical with a pointed tip. They are typically 20-40 cm long & 7-12 cm wide. These leaves are vivid green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The leaves grow in a spiral arrangement at the tips of the branches. The broad leaves enable the tree to capture sunlight for photosynthesis.

Flowers: Represents love, immortality, and spiritual devotion. They are vibrant red-pink or reddish-orange in color with a yellow center. Each flower measures around 5-7 cm in diameter. Each flower has 5 waxy petals arranged in a spiral pattern.
